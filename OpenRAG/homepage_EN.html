OpenRAG is an open-source Python framework for RAG benchmark. The goal is to help the user finding the right RAG methods for his own use case! Let's get started!


OpenRAG is a tool for comparing RAG methods against your data and identifying the most suitable approach for your use case; it is not designed for deployment or industrialization.
